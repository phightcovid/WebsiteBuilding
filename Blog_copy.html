State NPI Timeseries


All graphs are interactive!
Turn fields on and off by clicking on them in the legend.
Hover over elements on the graph for more information.


In these graphs the black dots represent new daily cases normalized to the state population.
The gray line represents the seven day rolling average.
The x-axis color bar displays a score out of a maximum of 5 points used to represent the strength of current state public health interventions.
The darker the color the stronger the interventions that are in place. Our score algorithm can be found in our github repository.
